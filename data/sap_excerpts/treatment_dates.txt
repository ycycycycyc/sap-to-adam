Section 7.2.1 Treatment Start and End Dates

Treatment start date (TRTSDT) will be derived as the date of first
study drug administration as recorded in the Exposure (EX) domain.
Specifically, TRTSDT is defined as the minimum EXSTDTC value across
all exposure records for a given subject where EXDOSE > 0. If a
subject was randomized but never received study drug, TRTSDT will
be set to missing.

Treatment end date (TRTEDT) will be derived as the date of last
study drug administration plus one day (to reflect the end of the
dosing interval), based on the maximum EXENDTC value across all
exposure records where EXDOSE > 0. For subjects who discontinued
prematurely, TRTEDT will reflect the last actual dose taken, not
the planned end of treatment.

Both dates will be presented in ISO 8601 numeric format (YYYY-MM-DD)
as SAS dates.
