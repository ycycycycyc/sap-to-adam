Section 5.1 Analysis Populations

The Safety Population flag (SAFFL) will be set to "Y" for all randomized
subjects who received at least one dose of study drug. Receipt of study drug
will be determined from the Exposure (EX) domain, where a subject has at least
one exposure record with EXDOSE > 0. Subjects who were randomized but never
received study drug will have SAFFL set to "N".

The Intent-to-Treat Population flag (ITTFL) will be set to "Y" for all
randomized subjects, regardless of whether study drug was received. Randomized
status will be determined from the Randomization (RS) domain using the
randomization date variable RSDTC. Subjects without evidence of randomization
will have ITTFL set to "N".

The Per-Protocol Population flag (PPROTFL) will be set to "Y" for all
randomized subjects who received at least one dose of study drug and had no
major protocol deviations impacting efficacy analyses. Major protocol
deviations will be identified from the Protocol Deviations (DV) domain where
DVCAT = "MAJOR" and DVDECOD indicates an efficacy-impacting deviation.
